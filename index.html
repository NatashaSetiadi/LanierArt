Our Mission

The mission of the Lanier High School art department is to inspire, educate, and fully prepare our art students to become passionate artists whom have respect for the arts and the people who enjoy them. With a commitment to promoting strong ethical values and professional demeanor, we strive to foster a sense of togetherness among our students, staff, parents, and community. We are dedicated to promoting students' productive, creative and innovative participation in the world of visual arts as concerned and caring citizens of the global community.

Our Teachers

Tiffany Cole graduated from The University of Georgia with a degree in Art Education. She has taught art at Heard County High School, Collins Hill High School and now at Lanier High School. Currently, This is Mrs. Cole's 10th year teaching. She loves all things art; creating, experimenting and exploring new techniques, methods, and media. Mrs. Cole lives in Buford with her husband, Brian and two sons, Andy and Ethan.


Kristina Jaunais graduated from the University of North Georgia with a bachelor's degree in Art Education with a minor in Art History. She has taught at WH Spencer High School in Columbus, Georgia, and is now teaching at Lanier High School. This is currently Ms. Jaunais' second year teaching. She enjoys all aspects of art from experimenting with new techniques and creating finished pieces to investigating and learning the history behind works. Ms. Jaunais grew up in Dacula and currently lives in Buford.
