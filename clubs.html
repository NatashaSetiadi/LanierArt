National Art Honors Society

National Art Honors Society is a community service based organization with the focus on using art to improve our school and community. For art students 10th - 12th grade, maintaining a B average in art classes and must have 80% attendance to meetings.

Students MUST pay the membership of $20 on My Payments Plus by the end of October.
